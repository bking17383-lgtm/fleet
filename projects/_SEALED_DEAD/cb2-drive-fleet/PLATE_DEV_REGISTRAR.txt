.dev ≠ buy from Google
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Google **invented/operates the .dev registry** (the TLD).
They are NOT automatically your registrar.

You buy the name from a **registrar** → point DNS to **Cloudflare** → done.

Google Domains was sold to **Squarespace** — skip unless you love that UI.
