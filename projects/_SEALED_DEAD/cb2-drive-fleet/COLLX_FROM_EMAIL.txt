# CollX export is in your EMAIL — not on Drive yet

Lester and Cursor cannot read Gmail attachments directly.
You must save the file once (2 minutes).

## On Chromebook (Chrome browser — NOT Linux terminal)

1. Open Gmail
2. Find the email from CollX with your collection export (CSV attachment)
3. Click the attachment
4. Choose ONE:
   A) "Save to Drive" → pick folder: collx_inbox
   B) Download → open Chrome OS Files → drag file into Google Drive → collx_inbox

5. On phone baseball app: CollX tab → Scan Drive

OR on Chromebook: http://127.0.0.1:8002 → CollX tab → Scan Drive

## Folder created for you

Google Drive → collx_inbox/

Put ANY CollX CSV there. Cursor auto-finds it (any name).

## If you only have the email on phone

1. Open CollX export email on phone
2. Save attachment to Google Drive → collx_inbox folder
3. Refresh baseball app → Scan Drive

No collx_export.csv required — any CSV in collx_inbox works.
