# Sarah — voice sample (CB2 interim · Puppy not ready)
Updated: 2026-06-15

**What it is:** scripted appointment voice demo — **NOT an LLM**.  
Same smooth feel as mesh radio: browser mic + **speechSynthesis** (rate 0.95).  
Integrity loop: echo → confirm → bundle question → school rule sample.

## What Sarah will hear (scripted)

1. Intro — not Gemini, not guessing  
2. She says an appointment → **"I heard: … Is that right?"**  
3. Bundle one day? yes / no / ask  
4. School rule sample (peanuts) or skip  
5. Sample complete — explains real app vs demo
