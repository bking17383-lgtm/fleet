Google Keep vs fleet bus — plain answer
Updated: 2026-06-15

SHORT ANSWER
Keep is great for YOU + GL on the go.
Keep does NOT show up as a file on Drive — no extension, Linux cannot read it.
So by itself it does NOT reach CAPTN / puppy / CB1.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Why you can't see an extension
  Keep notes live inside Google's Keep app — not in MyDrive as .txt or .gdoc.
  They are NOT in the folder Linux mounts at /mnt/shared/GoogleDrive/MyDrive.
  (Only things like TALK.txt, IDEAS.txt, *.gdoc stubs appear there.)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

What works today (pick one)

A) Keep + one ritual (best if GL writes Keep for you)
   Pin one note titled:  → CAPTN
   When you finish talking, say to GL:
     "Copy the last line to Drive IDEAS.txt"
   OR when you sit down: open Keep → copy → paste into TALK.txt once.

B) Skip Keep for fleet — use TALK.txt
   Phone Drive app, or talk here in Cursor, or Linux: talk

C) Heavy — later
   keep-exporter or Takeout → sync Keep into MyDrive/keep_inbox/*.md
   CAPTN reads that folder. Needs setup once; not today unless you ask.
