Kill Seaport (Google restored junk — not our fleet)

On Chromebook (Chrome OS — not Linux):

1. Click the circle (Launcher) at bottom left
2. Find Seaport icon → right-click → Uninstall
   OR: Settings → Apps → Google Play Store → Manage Android preferences
       → Apps → Seaport → Uninstall

If it came from Play Games:
  Settings → Apps → Google Play Games → disable or uninstall old games

You do not need Seaport. It is not Brian's app. Safe to delete.

If it keeps coming back:
  Settings → Google → Backup → turn OFF "Restore apps" for new device setup
  (Google loaded ~20 years of stuff — you pick what stays)
