## One line

Purchase a portable, handheld inkjet printer gun in the $130–$150 price range for rapid on-the-go labeling, marking, and asset tracking across fleet and project hardware.

## Problem

Today, marking physical assets, labeling custom components, or printing batch codes on hardware (like custom agents, server parts, baseball collection items, or plumbing parts) requires printing labels via a stationary setup, which is slow and doesn't scale for rapid on-the-go physical tagging.

## Proposal

Research and acquire a compact, budget-friendly handheld inkjet printer gun (12.7mm print height, touchscreen interface, support for quick-drying solvent inks in multiple colors) costing between $130 and $150 to streamline on-site hardware labeling and catalog tagging.
