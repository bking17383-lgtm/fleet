# CAMEL v0.7 design — Home vs Road vs Race

**Status:** Brian-approved direction (2026-06-12)  
**Play today:** `cd ~/lester && ./run_camel.sh` (v0.6)

---

## Four camels = four philosophies

| Type | Humps | Path | Feel |
|------|-------|------|------|
| **Standard** | 1 | Stay on family land | Slow profits, health, marriage, kids — **live, love, thrive** |
| **Racer** | 1 (fast) | Al Kharid circuit | **Land + oddball artifacts** from pots; without a racer you don't expand |
| **Bactrian** | 2 | Silk Road expedition | Leave home ~20 days; **geography lesson**; era ≈ **time of Christ** |
| **Golden** | ? | Unknown | High dung; purpose TBD — late-game reveal |

**Land rule:** 5 camels per parcel. New parcel **$2–5M** if cash — but **racing is the realistic land path** for players who won't grind millions.

---

## Path A — No racer: slow home game

You stay on family land. Growth is **slow and deliberate**.

**Core tension:** dung profits vs health vs wife/kids vs sandstorms.

**Random life events (pleasantries)** — flavor + tiny mechanical nudges, e.g.:

- *"Someone bought English vitals at the market and wants to share!"* (+small coin or +health or lore snippet)
- Neighbor shares kebab recipe (+1 day market bonus)
- Kid draws a camel in the dust (+morale / loving kid buff)
- Traveler's tale from the east (teases Silk Road without requiring Bactrian yet)
- Fatima approves of your restraint (if you didn't race this week)

Events should feel **human**, not loot-box spam. Mostly good; occasional "interesting" (not punishing).

**No racer = no easy land.** You balance on starter parcel(s) until you afford a Racer or get lucky elsewhere.

---

## Path B — Racer: land + oddball artifacts

Race pots weighted toward:

| Reward | Role |
|--------|------|
| **Land parcels** | Primary expansion (jackpot) |
| **Oddball artifacts** | Inventory — weird desert finds, collx/estate energy |
| Coins | Still common |
| Military / books | Support systems (sandstorms, sell bonus) |

**Golden camel:** racing TBD until mystery is defined; may be dung-only specialist for now.

Champion upgrades stay expensive — you're building a **winner**, not a clicker.

---

## Path C — Bactrian: Silk Road (future slice)

**Tone:** Teach **geography** — real places, ~**1st century AD** (time of Christ).

- Player is a merchant who **leaves family**; home game **suspended or runs at risk** (~20 game-days).
- Can **return home**; cargo + stories + maybe artifacts.
- Route nodes (example west → east):  
  **Petra → Palmyra → Antioch → Damascus → Babylon → Merv → Samarkand → Kashgar → (Han frontier / Chang'an basin)**
- Each stop: 1–2 sentences **real geography + period fact** + one event (trade, tax, bandit, gift, storm).
- Not a heritage movie — **map in the head**, one node at a time.

**Return:** silk/tea/coins/weird item; family state resolved (frozen vs background — TBD).

## Land economy (confirmed)

- **5 camels max** per parcel — hard cap.
- Cash land: **$2M–$5M** (escalating) — intentional wall.
- **Race land drops** stay — that's the exciting path.
- Home-only players: tiny parcel forever unless they save forever or get a social/event land gift (rare).
